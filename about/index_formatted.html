Our Story

Building Lasting Roofs Through Expertise and Commitment

At Steelcrest Roofing, we began with a simple goal: to provide homeowners and businesses with reliable, high-quality roofing solutions they can trust. From our first project to hundreds completed, our team has grown with one principle.

01
2021—Building a Reputation

Steelcrest Roofing was founded by a small team of experienced roofers with a shared goal: deliver reliable roofing work without shortcuts. Early projects focused on residential repairs and small roof replacements.

02
2022 — Expanding Materials & Expertise

Steelcrest Roofing introduced metal and steel roofing systems to meet growing demand for long-lasting, low-maintenance solutions. Training and certifications became a priority to ensure compliance with manufacturer.

03
2023 — Entering Commercial Roofing

With increased experience and crew capacity, Steelcrest took on its first commercial projects, including office buildings and small industrial properties. This marked a major step in the company’s growth.

04
2024 — Strength Through Challenges

Despite industry disruptions, Steelcrest Roofing maintained steady operations by focusing on safety, scheduling efficiency, and essential repair services. Customer trust and repeat business continued to drive growth.

05
2025 — Trusted Roofing Partner

Steelcrest Roofing became a recognized name for reliable residential and commercial roofing. The team continued investing in advanced materials, training, and safety standards to deliver long-lasting roofing systems.

FAQs

Clear answers you can trust — find what you need about your roofing project.

How long does a roof replacement take?

Most residential roofs can be replaced within 2–5 days, depending on size, material, and weather conditions. We provide a clear timeline before starting the project.

What roofing materials do you offer?

We offer galvanized steel, zinc alloy panels, shingles, clay tiles, polycarbonate sheets, and copper panels.

Do you provide warranties?

Yes — all installations and materials come with warranty options for long-term protection.

How do I know if my roof needs repairs or replacement?

If you notice leaks, cracks, sagging, missing shingles, or rising energy bills, it’s time for an inspection.

Can I schedule a consultation online?

Absolutely — book your inspection directly through our website.

Do you handle emergency repairs?

Yes, we offer fast emergency repair services for urgent issues.
